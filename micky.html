Michael Jackson

Biography

Michael Joseph Jackson ONM was an American singer, songwriter and dancer. Dubbed the "King of Pop", he is regarded as one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century. He continues to be one of the highest selling artists of all time, and his influence and incredible popularity reached every corner of the globe. His publicity was unheard of at the time, and his concerts were the largest by a long shot. Despite controversies and an untimely death, his overwhelming influence is heard in artists today and he is still regarded as the “King of Pop”, and rightfully so.
